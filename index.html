Asava Fort

4.7 km away

Asava fort is located in Palghar on a hill about 1070 feet high. Palghar has eight forts divided into two groups - five sea forts that are along the coast to the west, and three hill forts on the sub-range of the Sahyadris to its east.


Mini Goa

4.6 km away

Mini Goa Palghar Lake is a serene getaway, offering tranquil waters and picturesque surroundings


Dhodhadi Waterfalls

12.6 km away

A 30-minute simple trek to Dhodhadi Waterfalls takes you through lush greenery and scenic trails. The journey ends with the refreshing sight and sound of cascading water, perfect for a serene escape


Kaldurg Fort

12.7 km away

Kaldurg Fort, perched on a hilltop near Palghar, offers stunning panoramic views and a glimpse into Maratha history. Its easy trek and natural beauty make it a favorite for adventure seekers and history enthusiasts alike
